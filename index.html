FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time for thumbnails and edits? ▼

For custom thumbnails, our standard delivery is 24 to 48 hours. Video edits generally take 2 to 4 days depending on length and complexity. We also offer express turnaround options in our order page if you need them faster.

How does the ordering process work? ▼

You can use our interactive Order Configurator to build your project and get a pricing estimate. When you submit the request, it generates a unique order summary code. Copy that code and paste it in a ticket in our Discord server to lock in details and start work immediately!

Do you code custom Minecraft plugins? ▼

Yes! We have full-stack Java developers on our team. We can write custom Spigot, Paper, or Velocity plugins tailored exactly to your server's custom economy, mechanics, or mini-games.

What forms of payment do you accept? ▼

We accept secure payments through PayPal, credit/debit cards, and Stripe billing links which will be generated and sent to you during our onboarding ticket in Discord.
